Process

A straightforward, professional workflow that builds trust and keeps expectations crystal clear.

1) Walkthrough & Quote We review the scope, surfaces, and finish options—then provide a clear estimate.
2) Prep That Matters Protect floors/furniture, patch and sand, caulk where needed, and prime for best adhesion.
3) Paint & Quality Checks Even coats, clean edges, and consistent coverage—with touchups handled before final walkthrough.
4) Final Walkthrough We confirm everything looks right, clean up thoroughly, and leave your space looking sharp.
